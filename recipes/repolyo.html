Ginisang Repolyo with Egg

Description

This one is a great go-to when you want something heathy, but is at the same time within budget. We make use of a lot of affordable ingredients, some of which you can even take out of the equation because they’re optional.

Ingredients:

  • Cabbage
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Salt
  • Groud Black Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Cooking Oil

Steps:

  1. Preparing the cabbage
  2. Cooking the eggs
  3. Sautéing the vegetables
  4. Seasoning the mixture
  5. Combining the eggs with the rest of the ingredients
